1. Overview

Free My X ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy while providing a data liberation service. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our Service.

We facilitate access to your public X data but do not store or retain your personal data beyond what is necessary for authentication and service operation.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Authentication Information

  • OAuth Tokens: We temporarily store OAuth access tokens to facilitate authorization between you and third-party applications.
  • X User ID: We store your X user ID to identify authorized users.
  • Authorization Status: We maintain records of which users have authorized our service.

2.2 Technical Information

  • Usage Analytics: We use Simple Analytics (privacy-first analytics) to understand service usage without tracking individuals.
  • Error Logs: We may log technical errors for service improvement (anonymized where possible).
  • IP Addresses: Temporarily collected for security and rate limiting purposes.

2.3 Information We DO NOT Collect

  • We do not store your X posts, tweets, or content
  • We do not access or store private messages
  • We do not collect personal information beyond what's necessary for OAuth
  • We do not use tracking cookies or invasive analytics

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Service Operation

  • Authentication: To verify your identity and authorization status
  • API Access: To provide third-party applications with authorized access to your public X data
  • User Management: To maintain a list of authorized users for third-party verification

3.2 Service Improvement

  • Analytics: To understand usage patterns and improve our service
  • Security: To detect and prevent abuse or unauthorized access
  • Support: To provide technical support when needed

4. Information Sharing and Third-Party Access

When you authorize our service, you are explicitly granting third-party applications access to your public X data. Here's what happens:

  • Direct API Access: Third parties can directly access your public posts, profile information, and other public data
  • Data Storage: Third parties may store your data on their own servers indefinitely
  • Data Usage: Third parties may use your data for any lawful purpose, including commercial use

4.1 What We Share

  • Authorization Status: We confirm to third parties whether specific users have authorized our service

  • User Lists: We may provide lists of authorized users to approved third-party applications

4.2 What We Don't Share

  • We don't sell personal information to data brokers
  • We don't share private or non-public information
  • We don't provide access to OAuth tokens or authentication credentials

5. Data Security

  • Encryption: All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • Access Controls: We implement appropriate access controls for our systems
  • Token Security: OAuth tokens are stored securely and rotated as needed
  • Minimal Storage: We store only the minimum data necessary for service operation

6. Data Retention

  • Authorization Records: Stored until you revoke authorization or delete your account
  • OAuth Tokens: Refreshed and updated as needed, old tokens are discarded
  • Analytics Data: Anonymized analytics are retained for service improvement
  • Error Logs: Typically retained for 30-90 days for debugging purposes

7. Your Rights and Controls

7.1 Access Control

  • Revoke Authorization: You can revoke authorization at any time through our platform
  • Account Deletion: You can request deletion of your authorization records
  • Data Portability: You can request information about your stored data

7.2 Important Limitations

  • Third-Party Data: We cannot control or delete data already accessed by third parties
  • Public Data: Data you've made public on X remains accessible through other means
  • Cached Data: Third parties may have cached copies of your data

8. International Data Transfers

Our service may involve data transfers across international borders. By using our service, you consent to such transfers. We implement appropriate safeguards for international data transfers where required by law.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
