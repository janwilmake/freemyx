IETF AI Content Preferences Standard

The Problem

AI companies harvest public content without clear consent or compensation. Users have no control over how their data is used for training, search, or inference.

The Solution

The IETF is developing a standard for content usage preferences that lets creators specify exactly how their content can be used by AI systems.

IETF Scope Hierarchy:
tdmText and Data Mining
aiAI Training
genaiGenerative AI Training
searchSearch Indexing
inferenceAI Inference

Example Content-Usage Header:

Content-Usage: tdm=n, ai=n, search=y, inference=y

This allows search indexing and AI inference, but prohibits training data mining.
